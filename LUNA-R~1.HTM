Luna and the
Rainbow Jar

A Story About Feelings ✨

Ages 3–7  ·  12 Pages  ·  Interactive

🌈 For every feeling you feel

Luna felt happy when she danced.
Sad when it rained.
Angry when things went wrong.
Scared of the dark.
And brave when she tried something new.

Luna felt everything.

One morning, Luna found something magical in the garden.
A glass jar — empty and glowing.

"What are you?" Luna whispered.
The jar sparkled in reply. 🌟

💛 Happy

Luna ran to the sunny meadow.
She danced and twirled and laughed out loud.

POP! A golden bubble of HAPPY
floated right into the jar! ✨

💙 Sad

Then it began to rain.
Luna sat by the window and watched each drop fall.
A tear rolled quietly down her cheek.

PLOP! A soft blue drop of SAD
fell gently into the jar. 💧

❤️‍🔥 Angry

At dinner, Luna's tower of peas toppled over.
"NO!" she cried,
her cheeks burning red-hot.

WHOOSH! A fiery flame of ANGRY
swirled right into the jar! 🔥

💜 Scared

That night, the lights went out.
Luna hugged her blanket tight.
Her heart went thump... thump... thump.

SHIMMER! A purple mist of SCARED
settled softly into the jar. 🌙

🧡 Brave

But Luna took a deep breath.
She reached for her flashlight.
"I can be brave," she whispered.

GLOW! An orange spark of BRAVE
leapt into the jar! ⚡

A RAINBOW! 🌈

Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple...
All her feelings together made
something magical.

Luna carried her rainbow jar to her friends.
She opened the lid, and rainbow light
spilled everywhere! 🌈

"How did you make it?" they asked.
"I felt everything," said Luna,
"and kept it safe."

Every feeling is real. 💛 Every feeling is yours. All of your feelings make you beautifully, wonderfully YOU. ✨
