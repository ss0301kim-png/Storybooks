Ages 4–8 · 11 Pages · Interactive

🌙 Friendship starts with one brave hello

Every night, Sam heard it.
A sound from under the bed.

Scratch. Shuffle. Sniff.

Sam pulled the blanket up high.
"There's a MONSTER down there!" Sam whispered.

But was the monster scared too?

👾 Meet Moo!

Under Sam's bed lived a monster.
His name was Moo.

Moo was round and green and very, very shy.
He had big eyes that were almost always wide with WORRY.

Because Moo was scared of children.

😰 Moo's Big Fear

Moo went to Monster Support Group every Tuesday.
"My name is Moo," he said. "And I am... afraid of children."

All the monsters nodded. "Children are VERY loud!"
"And they turn the lights on!"

"And they smell like peanut butter," Moo whispered. 🥜

👋 The First HELLO

One night, Sam was EXTRA brave.
Sam grabbed a flashlight and leaned over the edge of the bed.

"HELLO? Is someone there?"

The flashlight found two big green eyes.
Blinking. Trembling. TERRIFIED.

The monster was more scared than Sam! 😳

🤝 "My Name is Moo"

"Don't be scared!" said Sam.
"I won't hurt you. What's your name?"

A very small, very shaky voice came from the dark.
"...M-Moo."

Sam said, "Hello, Moo. I'm Sam. Are you scared of me?"
"...A little," said Moo. "You smell like peanut butter." 🥜

🌟 Two Scared Friends

They sat up together all night long.
Moo told Sam about monster things — dark corners, squeaky floors, loud noises.

Sam told Moo about kid things — school tests, thunderstorms, the dark.

"We're both scared of stuff," said Sam.
"Maybe that's why we're going to be friends," said Moo. 💛

Best Friends! 👾💛

When the sun came up, Moo hid again — monsters can't be seen in daylight.
But every night, he'd come back.

And Sam was never scared of the dark again.
Because a friend was right there in it. 🌙

The scariest thing wasn't the dark. 🌙 It was being alone in it. 💜 Moo wasn't a monster. He was just shy. 👾 And now — he had a friend. 💛 🌟 THE END 🌟
