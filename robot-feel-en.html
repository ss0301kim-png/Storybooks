"What... are FEELINGS?" he wondered.

💛 What is HAPPY?

Bolt asked his friend Lily: "What is HAPPY?"

Lily spun around and laughed out loud.
"Happy feels like sunshine inside your chest!"

Bolt's lights flickered — and turned YELLOW. ✨

💙 What is SAD?

"What is SAD?" asked Bolt.

Lily showed him a flower that had wilted in the rain.
"Sad feels like rain inside your heart," she said softly.

A single blue drop rolled down Bolt's metal cheek.
His lights turned BLUE. 💧

❤️ What is ANGRY?

Their ice cream fell — SPLAT! — on the ground.
Lily's face went red. "I'm SO ANGRY!" she cried.

"Angry feels like fire inside your belly," she explained.

Bolt's lights turned RED. He felt it too. 🔥

💜 What is SCARED?

Thunder crashed. Lightning flashed.
Lily grabbed Bolt's arm and held on tight.
"Scared feels like your heart wants to run away," she whispered.

Bolt's lights turned PURPLE.
He held Lily's hand a little tighter. 🌩️

💚 What is BRAVE?

Lily showed Bolt the bridge she was afraid to cross.
"Watch," she said. She took one step. Then another.

"Brave doesn't mean not scared," said Lily.
"It means doing it ANYWAY."

Bolt's lights turned GREEN. He stepped forward too. 🌟

RAINBOW! ✨

Bolt looked at his chest panel.
Yellow. Blue. Red. Purple. Green.
All the feelings — glowing together.

"Is this... what it feels like to be ALIVE?"

Bolt turned to Lily.
"Lily... I think I feel something right now."

"What?" she asked.

Bolt's lights glowed the warmest gold he'd ever shone.
"I feel... GRATEFUL. For you."

And then — for the very first time — Bolt gave a hug. 🤖💛

You don't need gears to feel. 🤖 You don't need circuits to care. 💜 All you need is an open heart — and someone to share it with. 💛 THE END 💛
