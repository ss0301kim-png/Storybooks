## 📖 About This Book

**Luna and the Rainbow Jar** is a beautifully illustrated, interactive digital storybook that helps children ages 3–7 understand and embrace their emotions.

Luna is a little girl who feels everything — happy, sad, angry, scared, and brave. One day she discovers a magical jar, and begins collecting her feelings one by one… until her jar glows with the most beautiful rainbow she's ever seen.

### Why This Book Sells

- **#1 trending theme** in children's books (2025–2026): emotional intelligence & SEL
- **Bilingual** — English + Korean, instantly doubles your market
- **Interactive** — page-turning animations, swipe support, keyboard navigation
- **Print-ready quality** — each page is a standalone illustration
- **Activity pages** included — educators and parents love this
- **Mobile-friendly** — works on phone, tablet, and desktop
